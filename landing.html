The Core Pillars

A whole kingdom in a single HTML file.

Every sprite, enemy, and particle is drawn procedurally on canvas — no sprite sheets, no engine, no frameworks. Just raw JavaScript.

⚔

Four tiers of weaponry

Start with your fists. Earn an Axe, forge a Sword, and unlock the legendary Flame Hammer — a charged attack that unleashes fire particles and shakes the screen.

🏰

Build, upgrade, defend

Construct walls, farms, barracks, and forges. Hire soldiers and archers from a Refugee Camp. Upgrade your outpost from a lonely tower into a two-story fortress town.

🌙

Blood Moon waves

When the moon turns red, enemy scavengers, archers, and — occasionally — a boss-tier Emerald Mecha Dragon descend from the void portal.

🔥

Real particle physics

Fire embers, meteor trails, leaf drift, arrow tracers, and laser beams — every effect is a hand-tuned particle system running at 60fps.

📱

Desktop & mobile

Full glassmorphic touch control overlay on mobile. WASD + mouse on desktop. One codebase, zero compromises.

The Gameplay Loop

A rhythm you'll learn in a minute. Master in hours.

Day and night cycle. Gather, spend, fight, rebuild. Every wave ratchets the tension — every sunrise rewards the careful.

01/

Chop trees for wood. Hire workers from the Refugee Camp. Watch ladybugs crawl over the flowers while the sun rises.

The Philosophy
