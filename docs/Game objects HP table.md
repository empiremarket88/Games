TIP

Defensive Strategy: Use Soldiers (22 DMG) to hold the front line while Archers (25 DMG) provide fire from safety. The Hunter is best used for economy (Animal hunting) but can defend itself with 20 DMG javelins if cornered!

IMPORTANT

Player Power: Upgrading to the Sword (55 DMG) or Hammer (100 DMG) significantly increases your ability to clear waves manually, especially when combined with the 75 DMG Fireball skill.
