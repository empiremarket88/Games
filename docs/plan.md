# Pocket Kingdom - Project Roadmap

## 📅 Version 1.8: Inferno State (Current)
*Status: Completed*

- **Flame Hammer**: Multi-tier weapon system with charged fireball capability.
- **Inferno Graphics**: Additive blending, procedural heat tremors, and particle embers.
- **NPC Dialogue 2.0**: Proximity-based social interactions and improved speech bubble aesthetics.
- **Dynamic World**: Meteor showers and Blood Moon event triggers.

## 🏹 Version 1.9: Fauna & Frontiers (In Progress)
*Phase: Implementation*

- **Hunter Specialization**:
    - [x] **Pike Combat**: Ranged animal harvesting and enemy defense.
    - [ ] **Meat-Hauling AI**: Intelligent gathering and return-to-base logic.
    - [ ] **Visual Polish**: Unique "Hauled Items" sprites for carrying meat/hides.
- **Kingdom Economy**:
    - [x] **Meat Resource**: Sellable item at the Merchant shop.
    - [ ] **Economy Balance**: Resource scarcity vs. Merchant pricing (3 Gold baseline).
- **Environment & Bosses**:
    - [x] **Wyrmwing Dragon**: High-altitude procedural flying boss with continuous fire breath (Waves 3 & 7).
    - [ ] **Monarch Deer**: High-fidelity procedural wildlife animation (pending redesign phase).
    - [x] **Animal Redesign (Mockup)**: Gallery tool created for candidate selection.

## 🏰 Version 2.0: Siege & Sovereignty (Conceptual)
*Phase: Planning*

- **The Iron Wall**: Destructible defensive structures and repair systems.
- **Siege Engines**: Catapults and rolling towers for enemy waves.
- **Taxation System**: Automatic gold generation from Level 3 Farms.
- **Biome Expansion**: Frozen Tundra and Scorched Earth layered environments.
